About Eli Hills High School

A premier educational institution committed to academic excellence and character development

Principal's Welcome

Welcome to Eli Hills High School, where we have been nurturing young minds for over two decades. Our commitment to excellence in education and character formation has made us one of Uganda's premier secondary institutions.

We believe every student has unique potential, and our dedicated faculty works tirelessly to help each student achieve their dreams and aspirations.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures young minds and prepares students for global citizenship

Our Vision

To be the leading secondary school in Uganda, recognized for academic excellence and character formation

Our Values

Excellence Integrity Innovation Respect Service
